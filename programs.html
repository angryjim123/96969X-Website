Trello

As requested by our teacher, we were required to keep track of our work process through Trello. The main point of this is for us to be familiar with the Agile system. At first, we find it annoying to keep track of every little detail and moving the cards from To Do list to Doing list. However, we got used to the system. Agile increased our efficiency. Every one of us knew what to do from the start of class.
